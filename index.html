Jamal Nazrul Islam

1939 - 2013

About

  • General Relativity
  • Rotating Fields in General Relativity
  • Black hole theory
  • Cosmology equation
  • An Introduction to Mathematical Cosmology

Life Events

  • 1939 - Jamal Nazrul Islam was born on 24 February 1939 in Jhenaidah, East Bengal.
  • 1959 - In 1959, he got his Honors in Functional Mathematics and Theoretical Physics from Cambridge University.
  • 1960 - He completed his Masters in 1960.
  • 1964 - 1968 - Obtained his PhD in applied mathematics and theoretical physics from Trinity College, Cambridge in 1968
  • 1967 until 1971 - Worked in the Institute of Theoretical Astronomy (later amalgamated to Institute of Astronomy, Cambridge).
  • 1973–1974 - Served as the faculty of Applied Mathematics of King's College London.
  • 1978 - Joined the faculty of City University London
  • 1982 - Completed DSc in 1982
  • 1984 - returned to Chittagong, Bangladesh
  • 2006 - He was made Professor Emeritus at the University of Chittagong
  • 2013 - Jamal Nazrul Islam died on 16 March 2013 in Chittagong, Bangladesh

Awards

  • 1985 - Gold award from Bangladesh Academy of Sciences
  • 1998 - Medal Lecture award from Accademia Nazionale delle Scienze
  • 1998 - Bangladesh National Award for Science and Technology
  • 2001 - Ekushey Padak
  • 2011 - Razzak-Shamsun Lifetime Achievement Award in Physics from the University of Dhaka
